# Sales Insight Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard was designed to help AtliQ Hardware gain deeper insights into their sales trends. The goal was to automate the process of gathering and visualizing sales data, reducing manual data handling, and unlocking previously unseen sales insights. This project helped the sales team identify performance patterns and focus on key areas for growth.

## Objective

Understand sales trends over time.

Identify top-performing products, regions, and sales channels.

Automate manual data gathering processes to save time and improve efficiency.


## Dashboard Features:
Sales Overview: Provides a high-level summary of total sales, number of orders, and overall revenue trends.
Sales by Region: Displays the geographical distribution of sales, helping to identify high-performing regions.
Top Products: A breakdown of the top-selling products based on revenue and units sold.
Sales Trend Analysis: Visualization of sales over time, identifying peak sales periods and trends.
Customer Insights: An analysis of customer behavior, repeat purchases, and segment distribution.


## Key Insights Unlocked:
Regional Performance: The North and West regions consistently outperformed other areas, contributing to over 60% of total revenue.
Seasonality: Significant sales peaks were observed during the winter months, aligning with holiday seasons and promotions.
Top Products: Hardware accessories and peripherals showed higher sales volumes than core products, suggesting an opportunity for expanding product lines.
Process Efficiency: Automated the manual sales data gathering process, reducing time spent on data preparation by 20%.



## Conclusion:
This Sales Insights Dashboard has empowered AtliQ Hardware to take data-driven decisions by giving them visibility into their sales performance across various dimensions. By automating data gathering, the company has saved valuable time and can now focus on strategic improvements.
